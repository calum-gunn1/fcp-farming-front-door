Version 1 Round 2: 6th - 11th Mar

Context

We focused initially on messages, tasks and contact preferences as priority areas to explore.

We used the problem statement 'Unable to manage communications' that we wrote based on our previous research. Using this problem statement, we created the following How might we statements to guide our design thinking:

  • How might we make our communication with users more consistent/streamlined?
  • How might we ensure the right people get the right messages?
  • How might we stop users feeling overwhelmed by contact from Defra?
