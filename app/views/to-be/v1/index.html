Ideas and hypotheses to test

How might we make our communication with users more consistent/streamlined?
Idea Hypotheses
We could have a record of events/comms in the service, but users receive the messages by email If we send users message content by email instead of forcing them to log in to the service to read their messages
Then users will be less frustrated as there are less steps to read the message
We'll know this works people tell us they prefer to receive message content within an email without having to log in to the service

If we display all email content sent to users in a history/repository within the service,
Then users will feel reassured that they can find past messages from Defra, in case they need to refer back to anything
We'll know this works users show they can confidently refer to their message history
How might we stop users feeling overwhelmed by contact from Defra
Idea Hypotheses
We could allow users to filter messages by for info and for action If we allow users to sort messages by 'for action', and 'for information'
Then they can organise their messages by priority
We'll know this works they're able to more quickly find the things they need to do
We could let users choose how they receive notifications from Defra If we allow users to choose which channel they receive notifications on
Then they can use the channel that they use regularly and feels natural
We'll know this works when they are not frustrated by having to search for notifications or miss them
We could let users choose what updates from Defra they want to be notified about If we allow users to choose which updates from Defra they want to be notified about
Then they will not be overwhelmed with irrelevant updates that they're not interested in and cause stress or they could choose to not receive any
We'll know this works when users feel they are only receiving updates they want to receive.
We could let users choose how often Defra notifies them about things that don't require them to take action If we allow users to choose how often Defra contacts them about updates that don't require them to do anything
Then they can plan and manage how they deal with these communications
We'll know this works when users don't feel overwhelmed by irrelevant information and can manage this information
