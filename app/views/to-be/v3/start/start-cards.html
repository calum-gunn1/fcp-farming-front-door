There are two ways to sign in to your rural business account because some features are now in our new service.

Related content

Funding for farmers, growers and land managers

  • Sign in to the Manage schemes and grants service:

    • view and update your personal and business details
    • view messages
    • manage the:
      • Animal Health and Welfare scheme
      • Sustainable Farming Incentive (SFI)
      • Calf Housing for Health and Welfare grant

  • Sign in to the Rural Payments service to manage:

    • land and maps
    • Countryside Stewardship (CS)
    • who has permission to act on behalf of your business
    • messages about land and maps, CS and permissions

Register for an account

Register for an account by calling the Rural Payments helpline.

You'll be sent a Customer Reference Number (CRN) which you can use to sign in.

Rural payments helpline
Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
Telephone: 03000 200 301
Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
Find out about call charges

Find out more about how to register and how to give someone else permission to act your behalf.

Before you start

There's a different way to apply for rural payments and grants if:

  • your farm is in Scotland
  • your farm is in Wales
  • your farm is in Northern Ireland
