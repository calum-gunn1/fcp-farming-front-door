Claim funding to improve animal health and welfare

Use this service to claim funding for a vet to visit your farm to:

  • improve the health and welfare of your livestock
  • help reduce diseases

If you're eligible, you can claim funding for:

  • animal health and welfare reviews
  • endemic disease follow-ups
Start now

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password
  • endemic disease follow-ups

You need to have completed a biosecurity assessment if you are claiming for a follow-up for:

  • beef cattle
  • pigs

Get help with your claim

If you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim, contact the Rural Payments Agency and give your agreement number.

Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

Telephone: 03000 200 301

Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except public holidays
