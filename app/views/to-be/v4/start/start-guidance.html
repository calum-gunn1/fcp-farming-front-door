Guidance Manage your land and farm businesses service

Find out what you can use the service for now, and what you'll be able to use it for in the future.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency

Applies to England

Contents

  1. What you can use this service for now
  2. What you'll be able to use this service for in the future
  3. Before you start
  4. Contact us if you need help

'Manage your land and farm businesses' is a new service. It will provide a simple way to access a range of Defra services from a single account.

What you can use this service for now

You can use the service to:

  • manage claims for the Animal health and welfare review and Endemics follow-ups
  • access the existing Rural Payments service to manage schemes and carry out land and farm business tasks

What you'll be able to use this service for in the future

In future you'll be able to access more land and farm business tasks. This may include:

  • applying for or managing schemes such as Countryside Stewardship, Sustainable Farming Incentive, Animal health and welfare review or Endemics follow-ups
  • finding other funding opportunities
  • managing your land and maps
  • viewing your messages and payments
  • managing your account including personal and business details, contact preferences and who has permission to act on behalf of your business

Read more about plans for this service.

Before you start

You'll need a Rural Payments service (RPS) account to sign into this service.
