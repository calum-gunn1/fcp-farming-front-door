What you need to do next

  1. Contact the vet to arrange a review and give them the agreement number.
  2. Read the guidance about what happens on an animal health and welfare review. The guidance explains what you must ask the vet to do and what's optional.
  3. Ask the vet to read the guidance on how to do an animal health and welfare review and endemic disease follow-up. This is important because the vet needs to follow the guidance closely so that you are eligible to claim.
  4. After the vet has completed the review, you can claim your funding. Read the guidance on how to claim funding to improve animal health and welfare.

  • decide you do not want a review after you have accepted
