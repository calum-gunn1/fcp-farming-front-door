Thank you for your animal health and welfare review claim. So we can consider your claim, we need a copy of the vet's summary of the visit together with the following information for the registered livestock keeper:

For all species

  • the agreement number
  • the name of the business where the review took place
  • the single business identifier (SBI)
  • the date of the vet's last visit to the farm for the review
  • the type of livestock
  • confirmation the minimum number of animals required were on the farm during the review
  • confirmation the vet completed the testing in accordance with the review's disease and endemic conditions testing requirements
  • the date that sampling was completed
  • the unique reference number (URN) for laboratory test results
  • the vet's name and Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons (RCVS) number
  • confirmation the vet has given you a written report
  • the review summary sheet has been signed and dated by the vet
  • the test results (positive or negative)

What you need to do

Please send us this information within 10 working days from the date of this email. We can't progress your claim until we receive it.

You can scan and attach the documents in an email and send them to us at ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk. Or you can post them to the address at the bottom of this email. Use 'AHWR - Animal Health and Welfare Review' as the subject heading and put your SBI and AHWR claim number on everything you send us.

More information

You can read more information, on the How to apply for an animal health and welfare review of livestock page on GOV.UK.

If you have any questions about this email

You can email ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk. Use 'AHWR - Animal Health and Welfare Review' as the subject heading and tell us your SBI and Agreement number and Claim number if applicable which are at the top of this email. Or, you can call us on 03000 200 301 (Monday to Friday, 8.30am to 5pm).
