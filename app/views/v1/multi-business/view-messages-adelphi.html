Sustainable Farming Incentive - Agreement confirmation for IRISH BROS 02 January 2024

Business name: IRISH BROS

Agreement type: Sustainable Farming Incentive

SBI: 106811153

Agreement reference: 1664429

Thank you for accepting your agreement offer.

Your SFI agreement will start from 01 November 2024. It comprises:

  • your agreement document, which you can view in the Rural Payments service
  • your agreement's terms and conditions
  • the SFI actions you have chosen

You can find more information about your SFI agreement, including your agreement's terms and conditions and the details of the SFI actions, in the SFI handbook (search GOV.UK for 'SFI handbook').

Upgrading your SFI agreement

You can upgrade your SFI agreement to add more SFI actions and land. To do this, you must submit an 'upgrade request' towards the end of the first and second years of your 3-year SFI agreement. This must be done by the relevant deadline. We'll tell you what date that is.

Read the information about requesting changes to your SFI agreement in the SFI handbook to find out more about upgrading your agreement (search GOV.UK for 'SFI handbook').

What happens if you find you cannot comply with your agreement

Something may happen for reasons within or outside your control ('a change of circumstances') which means you cannot comply with your SFI agreement. If this is the case, you must tell us about this, in writing, as soon as reasonably practicable.

When you write to us, you should include:

  • your Single Business Identifier (SBI), which is shown above
  • your SFI agreement reference number, which you can find on your agreement document
  • as many details as possible about the change of circumstances, including specific details about the SFI actions you're having issues with

Read the SFI handbook to find out more about what happens if you find you cannot comply with your agreement (search GOV.UK for 'SFI handbook').

Contact us about SFI

To contact us about your SFI agreement, you can:

  • use the Rural Payments service to raise a query - select 'Create or view a query' to raise a new query
  • complete an SFI query form and email it to us (search GOV.UK for 'Contact the RPA about SFI' to find the query form)
  • email us at ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk - use 'SFI' as the subject heading and include your SBI
  • call us on 03000 200 301 (Monday to Friday, 8.30am to 5pm, except bank holidays)
  • write to us by post at Rural Payments Agency, PO Box 352, Worksop, S80 9FG
