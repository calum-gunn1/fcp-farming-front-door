Our records show that you have not yet submitted your Multi Annual Countryside Stewardship claim for the above agreement.

The deadline for the Rural Payments Agency (RPA) to receive your Multi Annual claim and supporting evidence is midnight on 17 May 2024.

Please sign in to the Rural Payments service and submit your claim as soon as possible.

If we do not receive a valid claim and any required supporting evidence by the deadline the following penalties will be applied, unless there are force majeure or other exceptional circumstances:

  • If we receive your valid claim form and any required evidence after midnight on 17 May 2024 but before midnight on 21 June 2024, we will apply a penalty of 1% of your total payment for this claim year for each working day that your claim is late;
  • If we receive your valid claim and any required supporting evidence after midnight on 21 June 2024, we will not progress your claim and you will not be paid for this claim year.

If you have more than one Countryside Stewardship agreement for your SBI, RPA must receive your Parent Claim declaration by midnight on 17 May 2024.

If you wish to discuss the content of this notification, please call RPA on 03000 200 301.
